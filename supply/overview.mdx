The Supply Planning module gives you full visibility into how well your supply chain meets demand. Monitor fulfillment rates, identify gaps, run simulations, and track inventory evolution across your network.

## What you'll see

### Order fulfillment chart
A stacked bar chart showing **fulfilled** (green) and **unfulfilled** (red) quantities for each time period. This gives you an immediate visual of supply performance.

You can double-click on any bar to drill down into the details for that specific period.

### KPI cards
Three summary cards at the top of the page, each with scenario comparison navigation:

| KPI | Unit | Direction |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **Order Fulfillment %** | Percentage | Higher is better |
| **Unfulfilled Value** | Currency | Lower is better |
| **Unfulfilled Volume** | Units | Lower is better |

Each card shows the value for your active scenario and arrows to navigate between scenarios for quick comparison.

### Inventory evolution chart
A line chart tracking inventory levels over time across your network. Use this to spot trends, seasonal patterns, and potential stockout risks.

### Order fulfillment details table
A detailed table breaking down fulfillment by product, customer, and facility. Expand rows to see granular fulfillment data at the SKU level.

## Page controls

| Control | Options | What it does |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Scope** | All, Secondary Sales, Primary Sales, Stock Transfers, Supply Sources | Filter by order type |
| **Granularity** | Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Fiscal Year | Change the time aggregation |
| **UOM** | Carton, Unit, Pallet, Kg | Change the unit of measure |

### Scope definitions

| Scope | What it includes |
|-------|-----------------|
| **All** | All order types combined |
| **Secondary Sales** | Customer orders (end-customer demand) |
| **Primary Sales** | Distribution center to warehouse orders |
| **Stock Transfers** | Inter-facility inventory movements |
| **Supply Sources** | Production and procurement orders |

## Key formulas

### Order fulfillment rate

```
Order Fulfillment % = (Fulfilled Quantity / Total Demand) x 100
```

A fulfillment rate of 100% means all demand was met. Anything below 100% indicates supply shortfalls.

### Unfulfilled metrics

```
Unfulfilled Volume = Total Demand - Fulfilled Quantity
Unfulfilled Value = Unfulfilled Volume x Unit Price
```

## Drill-down

Double-click on any bar in the fulfillment chart to filter the details table to that specific time period. A filter chip appears above the table showing your active drill-down. Click the **X** on the chip to clear the filter and return to the full view.

<Tip>
Use the **Scope** selector to isolate specific parts of your supply chain. For example, select "Secondary Sales" to focus on customer-facing fulfillment, or "Supply Sources" to check production output.
</Tip>
