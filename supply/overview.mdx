The Supply Planning module gives you full visibility into how well your supply chain meets demand. Access it via **Supply** under the IBP section in the sidebar.

## Filters

Before exploring, apply filters to focus your view:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Product** | Filter by product, product group, or all products |
| **Origin** | Filter by supplying location(s) or all origins |
| **Destination** | Filter by receiving location(s) or all destinations |
| **Date range** | Focus on a specific planning horizon |

## Order fulfillment chart

A stacked bar chart showing **fulfilled** (green) and **unfulfilled** (red) quantities for each time period. Hover over any bar to see a tooltip with:

- Order Quantity
- Fulfilled Quantity
- Unfulfilled Quantity
- Unfulfilled Value
- Order Fulfillment %

Double-click on any bar to drill down into the details for that specific period.

### Scope selector

| Scope | What it includes |
|-------|-----------------|
| **All** | All order types combined |
| **Secondary Sales** | Customer orders (end-customer demand) |
| **Primary Sales** | Distribution center to warehouse orders |
| **Stock Transfers** | Inter-facility inventory movements |
| **Supply Sources** | Production and procurement orders |

### Granularity

Switch between **Daily (D)**, **Weekly (W)**, **Monthly (M)**, **Quarterly (Q)**, and **Fiscal Year (FY)** views.

## KPI comparison cards

Three summary cards at the top of the page with scenario comparison navigation:

| KPI | Unit | Direction |
|-----|------|-----------|
| **Order Fulfillment %** | Percentage | Higher is better |
| **Unfulfilled Value** | Currency | Lower is better |
| **Unfulfilled Volume** | Units | Lower is better |

Each card shows the value for your active scenario and a delta comparison against other scenarios. Use the arrows to navigate between scenarios.

## Order fulfillment details table

A detailed table breaking down fulfillment by product, customer, and facility.

### View modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Aggregated** | Expandable hierarchy - drill down from totals to product level |
| **Atomic** | Flat individual records, paginated |

### Customizing columns

Click the **settings gear** icon to open the **Organize Detail Results** dialog:

- **Select Segments** tab - Choose dimensions: Brands, Sub Category, Product ID, Product Name, Product Range, Product Type, Strategic Segment, Sub Channel
- **Select Analytics** tab - Choose metrics: Fulfilled Quantity, Unfulfilled Volume (UoM), Root Cause

## Key formulas

### Order fulfillment rate

```
Order Fulfillment % = (Fulfilled Quantity / Total Demand) x 100
```

### Unfulfilled metrics

```
Unfulfilled Volume = Total Demand - Fulfilled Quantity
Unfulfilled Value = Unfulfilled Volume x Unit Price
```

## Drill-down

Double-click on any bar in the fulfillment chart to filter the details table to that period. A filter chip appears above the table. Click the **X** to clear the filter.

<Tip>
Use the **Scope** selector to isolate specific parts of your supply chain. Select "Secondary Sales" to focus on customer-facing fulfillment, or "Supply Sources" to check production output.
</Tip>
