SIMCEL automatically calculates over 51 KPIs from your simulation results. Each KPI is classified by category and direction (whether higher or lower values are favorable).

## Understanding KPI direction

| Direction | Meaning | Color when increasing |
|-----------|---------|----------------------|
| **Profit type** | Higher values are better | Green |
| **Lost type** | Lower values are better | Red |

## Finance KPIs

| KPI | Direction | Formula | Description |
|-----|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **COGS** | Lost | Direct from data | Cost of Goods Sold |
| **Transport** | Lost | Sum where cost type = Transport | Transportation costs |
| **Storage & Handling** | Lost | Sum where cost type = Storage & Handling | Warehouse costs |
| **Promotion** | Lost | Sum where cost type = Promotion | Promotional costs |
| **Medical Affairs** | Lost | Sum where cost type = Medical Affairs | Medical-related costs |
| **Administration** | Lost | Sum where cost type = Administration | Admin overhead |
| **Gross Profit** | Profit | Net FIE Sales - Distributor COGS | Profit after COGS |
| **Gross Margin %** | Profit | (Gross Profit / Net FIE Sales) x 100 | Gross profitability ratio |
| **Supply Chain Cost** | Lost | Transport + Storage & Handling | Total supply chain expenses |
| **DPM** | Lost | Supply Chain Cost + Promotion + Medical Affairs | Distribution, promotion & medical total |
| **DPM %** | Lost | (DPM / Net FIE Sales) x 100 | DPM as percentage of net sales |
| **DPMA** | Lost | DPM + Administration | Total operating expenses |
| **DPMA %** | Lost | (DPMA / Net FIE Sales) x 100 | DPMA as percentage of net sales |
| **EBIT** | Profit | Gross Profit - Transport - S&H - Promotion - Medical - Admin | Operating earnings |
| **EBIT %** | Profit | (EBIT / Net FIE Sales) x 100 | Operating margin |
| **COGS %** | Lost | (COGS / Net FIE Sales) x 100 | COGS as percentage of net sales |

## Dimensional breakdowns

All KPIs can be analyzed across the following dimensions:

| Dimension | Available levels |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Product** | Name, ID, Category, Range, Brand, Type, Strategic Segment |
| **Customer** | Name, Type, Channel, Region, City, Country |
| **Location** | Facility type (Factory, DC, Distributor, Customer) |
| **Time** | Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Half-year, Fiscal Year |

## KPI aggregation

KPIs are calculated using two methods:

| Method | Speed | Detail level | Best for |
|--------|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Lightweight** | Fast | Scenario-level totals | Dashboard widgets, quick overviews |
| **Atomic** | Detailed | Full dimensional breakdown | Drill-down analysis, pivot tables |

Lightweight KPIs load 50-70% faster and use 60% less data, making them ideal for dashboards and summary views. Atomic KPIs provide the full granularity needed for detailed analysis.
