## Step 1: Log in to your workspace

Navigate to your SIMCEL workspace URL and sign in using your organization credentials. SIMCEL uses single sign-on (SSO), so you can log in with your existing company account.

Once signed in, you will land on the **Homepage** where you can see key metrics, projections, and fiscal year achievements at a glance.

  <img src="/images/quickstart/login-page.png" alt="SIMCEL login page showing the SSO sign-in button and workspace selector" />
</Frame>

## Step 2: Select a plan

Use the **Plan Bar** at the top of the screen to select your active plan. SIMCEL supports two plan types:

- **Budget plan** - Annual planning cycle with a longer time horizon
- **Tactical plan** - Short-term operational planning for immediate adjustments

Click on the plan name in the top bar to switch between available plans.

<Frame caption="Screenshot: The Plan Bar in the top navigation showing plan name and type (Budget/Tactical)">
  <img src="/images/quickstart/plan-bar.png" alt="Top bar with the Plan Bar highlighted, showing the active plan name and a dropdown to switch plans" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Choose your scenario

The **Scenario Bar** displays your active scenarios. Each plan can have multiple scenarios to compare different planning assumptions:

- **Forecast Base** - The auto-generated baseline forecast (read-only)
- **Primary scenario** - Your main working scenario
- **Custom scenarios** - Additional what-if scenarios you create

Click on scenario chips in the top bar to toggle their visibility on charts and tables.

<Frame caption="Screenshot: The Scenario Bar showing colored scenario chips (Forecast Base, Primary, custom scenarios)">
  <img src="/images/quickstart/scenario-bar.png" alt="Scenario Bar with multiple colored scenario chips, some toggled on and some off" />
</Frame>

## Step 4: Explore your modules

Use the **sidebar menu** on the left to navigate between modules:

| Module | What you'll find |
| --- | --- |
| **Business Explorer** | High-level KPI dashboard with P&L summary |
| **Demand** | Demand forecasts, trends, and breakdowns |
| **Stock-In-Trade** | Inventory planning and safety stock analysis |
| **Supply** | Order fulfillment rates and simulation tools |
| **Events** | Event impact modeling and management |
| **CO2** | Carbon emissions tracking and trends |

## Step 5: Apply filters

Use the **filter bar** at the top to narrow your view:

- **Products** - Filter by specific products, brands, or categories
- **Customers** - Filter by customer, channel, or region
- **Date range** - Focus on a specific time window

Filters apply across all charts and tables on the current page.
