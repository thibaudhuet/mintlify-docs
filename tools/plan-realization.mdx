The Plan Realization tool helps you track how well your actual business performance aligns with what you planned. It's essential for measuring planning effectiveness and accountability.

## How to access

Navigate to **Tools > Plan Realization** from the sidebar.

## What it shows

A comparison between:
- **Planned values** - The KPI targets from your primary scenario
- **Actual values** - Real performance data imported into your workspace

## Key insights

- **On track** - Where actuals are meeting or exceeding the plan
- **At risk** - Where actuals are falling short of planned targets
- **Variance** - The absolute and percentage difference between plan and actual

## How to use it

1. **Monitor execution** - Check regularly to see if your business is tracking to plan
2. **Identify gaps early** - Spot deviations before they become significant
3. **Adjust plans** - Use insights to update your tactical plan or create corrective scenarios
4. **Report to stakeholders** - Provide evidence-based updates on planning accuracy

<Tip>
Combine Plan Realization insights with Forecast Accuracy data to understand whether gaps are due to forecasting errors or execution issues.
</Tip>
