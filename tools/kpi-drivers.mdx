title: KPI drivers

---

The KPI Drivers tool helps you understand how different KPIs relate to and influence each other. It displays a tree-like visualization of KPI dependencies.

## How to access

Navigate to **Tools > KPI Drivers** from the sidebar.

## What it shows

A visual map of how your KPIs connect:

- **Input KPIs** (drivers) feed into **output KPIs** (results)
- Follow the chain from revenue through costs to profit
- Understand which levers to pull to impact a specific metric

## Example driver chain

```
Gross FIE Sales
  └── - FIE Trade Expenses
       └── = Net FIE Sales
            └── - Distributor COGS
                 └── = Gross Profit
                      └── - Supply Chain Cost
                           └── - Promotion
                                └── - Medical Affairs
                                     └── = DPM
                                          └── - Administration
                                               └── = EBIT
```

## How to use it

1. **Identify root causes** - If EBIT is declining, trace back through the driver tree to find which input KPI is causing the change
2. **Impact analysis** - See which downstream KPIs will be affected when a driver changes
3. **Planning focus** - Determine which KPIs to prioritize in your planning based on their influence on final outcomes
