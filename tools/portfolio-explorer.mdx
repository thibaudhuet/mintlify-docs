The Portfolio Explorer provides an interactive sunburst chart that lets you explore your data across hierarchical dimensions. It's ideal for understanding composition and identifying where value is concentrated.

## How to access

Navigate to **Tools > Portfolio Explorer** from the sidebar.

## What it shows

A **sunburst chart** where:

- **Center ring** - The total across all data
- **Inner rings** - Top-level groupings (e.g., Brand, Region)
- **Outer rings** - Detailed breakdowns (e.g., Product, Customer)

Click on any segment to zoom in and explore that portion of the hierarchy in more detail. Click the center to zoom back out.

## Dimensions

Navigate through hierarchies such as:

- **Product hierarchy** - Category > Brand > Range > SKU
- **Customer hierarchy** - Region > Channel > Customer
- **Location hierarchy** - Country > City > Facility

## Use cases

- **Revenue concentration** - See which products or customers drive the majority of your revenue
- **Cost distribution** - Understand how costs are spread across your network
- **Portfolio balance** - Assess whether your business is overly dependent on a small number of products or customers
