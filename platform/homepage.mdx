The Homepage is the first screen you see when you log in to SIMCEL. It provides a high-level snapshot of your business performance.

## What you'll see

### Welcome message
A personalized greeting with your name and the current date.

### Key metrics widget
A summary of your most important KPIs at a glance. These metrics are pulled from your active plan and primary scenario.

### Projections chart
A visual representation of projected performance across your planning horizon. Use this to quickly spot trends and potential issues.

### Fiscal year achievement
Track your progress against fiscal year targets. The widget shows how your current trajectory compares to your annual goals.

<Tip>
The fiscal year start month is configured in your workspace settings. All fiscal year calculations across SIMCEL use this setting.
</Tip>

## Using the homepage

The Homepage is designed for quick orientation:

- **Check the pulse** - Glance at key metrics to understand current performance
- **Spot trends** - Use the projections chart to identify upward or downward movements
- **Track progress** - See how far along you are toward your fiscal year targets

From here, use the sidebar to dive into any specific module for detailed analysis.
