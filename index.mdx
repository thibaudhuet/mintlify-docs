SIMCEL is a SaaS Integrated Business Planning (IBP) platform that brings together your Demand, Supply, Finance, and Carbon planning into one unified workspace. Plan smarter, simulate scenarios, and make data-driven decisions across your entire supply chain.

## What is SIMCEL?

SIMCEL helps organizations align their business plans across four key pillars:

## Key capabilities

- **Scenario planning** - Create and compare multiple planning scenarios side by side
- **Event modeling** - Simulate the impact of promotions, market changes, and operational events
- **51+ KPIs** - Automatically calculated metrics covering demand, supply, finance, and emissions
- **Real-time simulation** - Run supply chain simulations and see results in minutes
- **Collaborative planning** - Work with your team using comments, tasks, and shared scenarios
- **Multi-currency support** - Plan across regions with automatic currency conversion
